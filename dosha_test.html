Dosha page for individuals seeking ayurvedic knowledge

Doshas refer to three cogntive impressions known as vata pitta and kapha. Each of three energies believed to circulate in the body and govern physiological activity, their differing proportions determining individual temperament and physical constitution. when unbalanced they can cause dispositions to particular physical and mental disorders.
